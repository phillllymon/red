GRAFFITI has access to two sources of personal information: your email address, which you provide upon signup, and what websites you visit while the extension is enabled. We use this information for no purpose other than the functionality of GRAFFITI. We may email you notifications if you are tagged in a post, recovery information if you forget your login information, or other updates about GRAFFITI. Posts may be deleted by GRAFFITI; for example - we may delete posts associated with webpages that are no longer live or, to save storage space, delete old posts.
